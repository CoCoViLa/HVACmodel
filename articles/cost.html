Component: Cost function

The Cost component implements the cost function.

One of it's properties is price of the electricity (kW-h). We suppose that all electric energy is transformed to heat energy. Also, usually we have price for (kW-h), but in our model we have (J), so we transform between them using 1 kW-h=3.6e6J.

The output value of Cost component is accumulated cost of the house heating/cooling. The input is the heat flow from heater/cooler.
