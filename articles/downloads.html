Version 1.0

CoCoViLa package for modeling and simulation of heating and cooling processes of house with air heating. The system consists of several components, such as: house, heater/cooler, controller, thermostat, etc.

The simulation results can help to choose parameters for comfort temperature, properties of the heater/cooler and modify the control algorithms.

Initially, this package was inspired by Simulink demo ”Thermal model of the house”.
