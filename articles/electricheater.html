Component: Air conditioner

The AirConditioner models the processes of heating and cooling air. AirConditioner can be described as air pump with constant air flow flowRate.

The temperature of heating heaterTemperature and cooling coolerTemperature elements are defined by user.

Default values of parameters are:

  • Heater temperature 50(C);
  • Cooler temperature 5(C);
  • Flow rate 1(kg/sec);

According to the signal the device choses between heating or cooling processes. Those processes are not linear. Both processes (heating and cooling) implements the same equation, although, for heating T is heater temperature, for cooling T is cooler temperature.

HF=(Theater-Troom)* FR * cair

where:

  • HF - heat flow (J/min);
  • Theater - heater temperature (C);
  • Troom - room temperature (C);
  • FR - air flow rate (kg/min);
  • cair - air heat capacity (J/kg-K);

The output value is heatFlow, thew inputs are air parameters: air heat capacity c, current indoor temperature roomTemperature and signal from Thermostat.
